# GigShield 🛡️
### AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Delivery Workers
> Protecting India's Swiggy & Zomato riders from income 
> loss caused by rain, floods & disruptions — automatically.

---

## 👤 Our Persona
We are building for **Food Delivery Partners** working 
on Swiggy and Zomato in Indian metro cities like 
Chennai, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

These riders earn ₹500–₹800/day but lose 20–30% of 
their monthly income when extreme weather or disruptions 
stop their work. Today, zero insurance products exist 
for them.

---

## 🎯 The Problem
Ravi is a Swiggy rider in Chennai who earns ₹18,000 
a month. Last July, Chennai received 3 days of heavy 
rainfall. Ravi lost ₹2,400 with no compensation and 
no safety net. He had no insurance, no platform 
support, and no government protection.

There are 15 million+ gig delivery workers in India 
facing this same reality every monsoon season.

---

## 💡 Our Solution
GigShield is an AI-powered parametric insurance 
platform that automatically detects weather 
disruptions in a rider's delivery zone and instantly 
transfers lost income compensation to their account 
— with zero forms, zero calls, and zero waiting.

When it rains heavily in Ravi's zone, GigShield 
detects it, approves his claim automatically, and 
sends ₹350 to his Paytm before he wakes up.

---

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model
Our premium is calculated weekly using AI:

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Base Premium | ₹49/week |
| Zone Risk Score | Based on historical flood/rain data per pin code |
| Weather Forecast | +/- ₹10 based on next 7 days forecast |
| Rider History | Discount for claim-free weeks |
| Final Range | ₹35 to ₹80 per week |

Gig workers think week to week — so does GigShield.

---

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers
GigShield automatically initiates a claim when:

1. 🌧️ Rainfall > 50mm/hour in rider's pin code zone
2. 😷 AQI > 400 (severe pollution) in rider's city
3. 🌡️ Temperature > 45°C for 3+ consecutive hours
4. 🌊 Official flood alert issued for rider's district
5. 🚫 Curfew or shutdown order in rider's zone

No human approval needed. System triggers instantly.
